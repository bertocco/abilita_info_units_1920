RECOVERY INSTRUCTIONS

Students can be absent and recover up to 3 lessons.

Who has not be present to one or more than 3 seminars (up to 3) can recover in two steps:

1. studying the slides of the seminars available on a github repository:
 https://github.com/bertocco/abilita_info_units_1920
and practicing with exercises proposed in the slides

2. carryng out, for each seminar, the exercises in the files (again available in the github repository):
	bash_1_recupero.txt
	bash_2_recupero.txt
	bash_3_recupero.txt
	python_1_recupero.txt
	python_2_recupero.txt
	python_3_recupero.txt
and sending solutions (executable scrips in text format) to the teacher by e-mail:
sara ‘dot’ bertocco ‘at’ inaf ‘dot’ it

The correctness verification of the proposed solutions will consist in a brief check of the proposed solutions and in running the scripts.
